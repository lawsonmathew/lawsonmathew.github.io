ABOUT

George Lawson Mathew

Technical Solutions Engineer at Epic

As a first generation student, born and raised in India for the first nine years of my life, I have had to grasp at the opportunities available to be where I was today. The most important opportunity was in high school, when I had the support of the Schuler Scholar Program. They helped enrich my life with opportunities for exposure and access to events I would never have had.

Since my education in the U.S. started, my hobbies have become more technology related. This included working with image manipulation softwares, video editing, animation, and programming. I am an avid fan of Tottenham Hotspur soccer team, a general sports enthusiast, and also like to cook.

I graduated from Tufts University in 2021 with a Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering and a minor in Computer Science. I studied abroad at University College London during my junior year focusing mainly on Mechanical Engineering courses.

I currently work at Epic Systems as a Technical Solutions Engineer, where I use my problem solving skills to support the Epic community within the billing module.
